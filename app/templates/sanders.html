Introduction

The following analyses performed on a sample of the 11958 comments retrieved from articles that referenced Bernie Sanders. After filtering out neutral comments (sentiment scores between -0.1 and 0.2) and comments that do not mention the candidate there are 1395 comments for the sentiment analyses. Bar graphs represented the distributaion of comments that fall under each topic. For the comments displayed, many of them are truncated to only show the first two sentences due to the limitation of the text box.
